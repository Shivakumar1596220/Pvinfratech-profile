PV INFRATECH is a leading provider of DGPS and Drone survey solutions across India. We are committed to delivering accurate, reliable, and timely geospatial data to support critical infrastructure and development projects, enabling our clients to build the future with confidence.

Our Core Services

🛰️ DGPS Survey

Utilizing advanced Differential Global Positioning System technology to achieve centimeter-level accuracy for large-scale and complex projects.

  • Land and topographic surveys
  • Route and corridor surveys
  • Volume and earthwork calculations
  • Boundary and demarcation surveys

🚁 Drone Survey

A safe, efficient, and cost-effective method for capturing high-resolution aerial data for a multitude of applications.

  • Aerial photography and videography
  • 3D modeling and photogrammetry
  • Digital elevation models (DEM & DSM)
  • Site progress monitoring and reporting
